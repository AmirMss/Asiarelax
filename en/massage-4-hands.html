Couples Massage & 4-Hand Massage

Couples Massage

The couples massage is a moment of sharing, trust and sensory awakening. Performed simultaneously by two therapists, it allows you to experience deep relaxation together in a soothing setting.

It is an ideal gift idea to offer a moment of wellbeing and closeness to your partner, a friend or a family member.

4-Hand Massage

With four hands, the brain cannot focus precisely on the movements and completely lets go. The result is absolute serenity and total body relaxation.

Two therapists work in perfect synchronisation to create a unique experience where every area of the body is stimulated simultaneously, multiplying the feelings of wellbeing.
