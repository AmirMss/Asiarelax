Relaxing Thai Massage

Traditional Thai massage is a preventive and therapeutic practice taught in Buddhist temples, particularly at the Wat Po school in Bangkok. This technique uses gliding and circular movements along the body's energy lines.

Our certified therapists combine traditional Thai techniques with hot essential oils for a deeply relaxing and revitalising experience.

Hot Oil Massage

Hot oil massage combines the benefits of heat with the properties of essential oils. Heat allows muscles to relax more quickly and deeply, while the oils nourish and hydrate the skin.
