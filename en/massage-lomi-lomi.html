Lomi Lomi Deep Tissue Massage Sessions

Lomi Lomi Massage

Lomi Lomi massage is ideal for those wishing to recentre themselves, restore body/mind balance and release external disturbances that trouble inner energy. Techniques include stretching, acupressure and deep kneading.

This massage works deeply on the muscles and fascia of contracted areas, using a "roller effect" to restore the body's normal mobility.

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep tissue massage uses slow, deep movements focused on the muscles and fascia of the most tense areas. This technique is particularly effective for chronic pain and deep tension.

Indications:

  • Stress relief
  • Lower back and joint pain
  • Rheumatism
  • Arthritis
  • Reduction of muscle inflammation
