Back, Leg, Foot, Face and Head Massage

Foot Massage

Your feet represent the entirety of your being. They contain more than 7,200 nerve connections linked to your entire body. Foot massage uses plantar pressure techniques to relieve the stress and tension accumulated daily.

This ancestral practice stimulates reflex zones and promotes blood circulation, providing a deep and lasting sense of wellbeing.

Head Massage

Head massage produces deep relaxation. It stimulates blood circulation and oxygenation of the scalp while releasing anti-stress hormones. This massage provides immediate relief and helps combat headaches, migraines and nervous tension.

Back Massage

The back is one of the most stressed areas of your body. Muscle tension accumulates there over time, causing pain and stiffness. Our back massage deeply targets tension areas to relax contracted muscles and provide lasting relief.

Leg Massage

Your legs bear the weight of your body throughout the day. Fatigue, prolonged immobility or physical exertion cause a feeling of heaviness. Leg massage activates blood and lymphatic circulation, restoring lightness and vitality to your lower limbs.
